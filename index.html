Stock Trader

Stock Trader is aiming to provide a zero % commission trading platform to the consumers. The startup product is a web application that will buy, sell, track & manage the portfolio. People who are still learning about the stock market and are interested in investing their money in it can safely experiment about their prediction.

Using this application, a user can simulate what it is like buying, selling and managing stocks without the stress and consequences of losing and mismanaging money.
